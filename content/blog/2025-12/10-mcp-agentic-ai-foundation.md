[Modal Context Protocol](https://modelcontextprotocol.io) is now part of [Agentic AI Foundation](https://aaif.io/) which is under **Linux Foundation**

The founding platinum members are as you can guess companies like Anthropic, Google, OpenAI, etc

And they claim the following goal:
>  "a neutral, open foundation to ensure agentic AI evolves transparently and collaboratively".
