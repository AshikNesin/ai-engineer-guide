Merriam-Webster has chosed [**Slop**](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/slop) as the word of the year

> digital content of low quality that is produced usually in quantity by means of artificial intelligence

It's something that was created for the purpose of engagement without human effort (or very less effort).
