Google AI studio now supports building **native** Android app for free.

For quick prototypes/app you can build it within the AI studio itself.

They also have device emulator as well so you can see the UI, playaround with it, etc.

Though when I tried it, it took quite some time to build a basic app, I guess that's something we'll have to expect when everything is built on Cloud.

To get started, select "Build an Android app" in the AI studio
