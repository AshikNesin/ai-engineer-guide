[Stainless](https://www.stainless.com) the SDK layer behind pretty much all the leading AI companies including OpenAI, Anthropic, etc is getting acquired by Anthropic

You just need to connect your OpenAPI spec with them, they take care of the rest for your SDK across all the different languages like TypeScript, Python, Go, etc.

> Anthropic created MCP to make agent connectivity possible. By bringing together the Stainless and Anthropic teams, the Claude Platform continues to push the frontier of developer experience and agent connectivity.

I guess it is more of acqui-hire.
