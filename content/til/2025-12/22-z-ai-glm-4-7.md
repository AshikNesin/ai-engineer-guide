z.ai team has released their latest LLM GLM-4.7 - primirily it has the following improvements:
 - Coding
 - Better UI than 4.6
 - Improved tool calling capabilities
 - Complex Reasoning (or at least that's what they're claiming)
 
 ![2025-12-22-at-23.39.33.png](https://images.nesin.io/f_auto,q_auto/qblog/AIEngineerGuide/2025-12/s2m2hnqidkh3rymgdjns)
