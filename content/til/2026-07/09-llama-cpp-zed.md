[Zed](https://zed.dev) has now support for [llama.cpp](https://llama.app/) which means now you can easily use local models within Zed with minimal effort.

## How to get started?
You can configure this in Zed preferences. 

AI -> LLM Providers -> llama.cpp

![2026-07-09-at-22.49.35.png](https://cdn.qblog.nesin.io/f_auto,q_auto/qblog/AIEngineerGuide/2026-07/vvchwaqyjw4uy1xzsohf)

Once you configure it, you should be able to use that model.
