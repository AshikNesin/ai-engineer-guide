I came across this video by Andrew regarding [FluidVoice](https://altic.dev/fluid) an open source alternative to something like Wispr Flow or Superwhisper.

Basically, the speech to text happens locally. It doesn't leave your device.

![2026-07-06-at-23.45.22.png](https://cdn.qblog.nesin.io/f_auto,q_auto/qblog/AIEngineerGuide/2026-07/bhfzexvfkpiz6tlflply)

## How to get started?

```shell
brew install --cask fluidvoice
```
