Similar to changing [effort](https://aiengineerguide.com/til/change-effort-in-claude-code/) while interacting with Claude Code, you can also configure `effort` in **SKILL.md or Slash command** as well.

Just add `effort: low | medium | high | max` (one of the value) in the frontmatter
