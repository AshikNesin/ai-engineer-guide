I came across this really high quality video about [Ilya Sutskever](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ilya_Sutskever) and history of OpenAI.

- History of neural network and the stagnation it had for decades
- Right from how Ilya started interacting with [Geoffrey Hinton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geoffrey_Hinton), [Alex Krizhevsky](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alex_Krizhevsky) to presenting [AlexNet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AlexNet) which was trained using GPU and how it outperformed all the image recogination models at that time. And it was the breakthrough at that time.
- His Google days
- Starting OpenAI as a non-profit company which was funded by Elon and it's culture
- Building GPT model as PoC
- How OpenAI was struggling to raise money because of non profit structure. And how Elon wanted to merge OpenAI with Tesla because of the cash crunch.
- Why Google didn't take any action with Attention is all you need paper (conflict of interest with Ad revenue)
- OpenAI getting funding from Microsoft (who had became a majority share holder)
- Sam Altman's way of running OpenAI - Like a traditional startup. Ship fast break things!
- How ChatGPT (which used GPT-3 model) got exploded in few days
- The drama which lead to Sam's firing at OpenAI and reinstating with more power
- Ilya leaving OpenAI to starting [Safe Superintelligence Inc](https://ssi.inc/)
