OpenAI has released their new general-purpose model **GPT 5.4**

Here are some of the key highlights:
- 1 million context window
- Steerability (guiding) in thinking process without starting from scatch
- State of art computer control. Good at navigating computer like a human
- Significant token efficiency & speed improvements
- Strong benchmark results

They've also released their [system card](https://deploymentsafety.openai.com/gpt-5-4-thinking)
