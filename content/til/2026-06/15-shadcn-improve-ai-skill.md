shadcn's [improve](https://github.com/shadcn/improve) skill helps you to audit your entire codebase and gives you suggestion on how to improve it.

It is recommended to use powerful models like Opus which will gather the needed context to generates the plan, you can use cheap models to get things done.

## How to use it?

```shell
npx skills add shadcn/improve
```
