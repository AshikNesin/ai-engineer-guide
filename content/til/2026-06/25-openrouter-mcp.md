## How to get started?
They provide this via remote MCP. You can add it just by adding the following remote server.

`https://mcp.openrouter.ai/mcp`

> When you trigger it, an OpenRouter consent page opens in your browser where you approve a dedicated key just for this connection, separate from your other keys. The key expires after 7 days and starts with a $10 spend cap (editable on the approval screen)
