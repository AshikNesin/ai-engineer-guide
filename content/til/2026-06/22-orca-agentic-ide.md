[Orca](https://www.onorca.dev/) IDE might be a good choice if you're working on large number of projects or uses lots of harness at the same time.

Instead of you switching around with multiple apps which does a certain thing. This app does most of the things like terminal, managing multiple agents, editor, git, browser, etc
